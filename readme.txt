== Installation ==

**Automatic Installation**

* The efficient way to install the plugin is through the WordPress dashboard:
1. Navigate to your WordPress dashboard.
2. Go to the "Plugins" section and select "Add New."
3. In the search bar, type "Child Support Calculator."
4. Locate the plugin in the search results and click on "Install Now."
5. Finally, click "Activate Plugin" to enable its functionality.

**Manual Installation**

*  For those who prefer a more hands-on approach, follow these steps:
1. Download the Child Support Calculator plugin from the official source.
2. Unzip the downloaded package to access the "child-support-calculator" folder.
3. Connect to your website's server using an FTP client or access your server's file manager.
4. Upload the "child-support-calculator" folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory on your server.
5. Once uploaded, return to your WordPress dashboard.
6. Navigate to the "Plugins" section and locate the Child Support Calculator plugin.
7. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

**Configuration**

* After activating the plugin, you can customize its settings:
1. In your WordPress dashboard, click on "Child Support Calculator" located in the "Dashboard Options" menu.
2. Adjust the settings to meet your specific requirements.

= How can I upgrade to the premium version? =

To access the premium version, simply make a purchase of this plugin. Afterward, you'll receive an email containing a link to download the premium version, along with the license key required for installation.

= Is it necessary to customize the calculator after installing it? =

Installing and customizing the calculator is hassle-free using the options panel. This enables you to seamlessly integrate it with your webpage's design.


= Will manual updates be required for the plugin? =

No, manual updates are not needed. Our Automatic Update feature ensures that the plugin remains up to date without requiring you to manually download or install anything.

= Who can benefit from this plugin? =

The Child Support Calculator (WPCSC) is specifically designed for lawyers and individuals within the legal community. By placing the calculator on your webpage, you can generate leads related to family law.

= How do I implement it? =

Implementation is straightforward. Utilize the user-friendly shortcode [WPCSC] to display the calculator. This shortcode showcases the impact on support percentage for families with one to six children.

= What guidelines does WPCSC adhere to? =

WPCSC follows the guidelines set by respective states. Currently, there are calculators available for Florida and North Carolina. You can choose to feature either one or both on different pages of your website.

= In which areas is this calculator applicable? =

This calculator is valid for 35 US states, including but not limited to: Alabama, Arizona, California, Colorado, Connecticut, Florida, Georgia, Idaho, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Louisiana, Maine, Maryland, Michigan, Missouri, Nebraska, New Jersey, New Mexico, New York, North Carolina, Ohio, Oklahoma, Oregon, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Carolina, South Dakota, Utah, Vermont, Virginia, Washington, West Virginia, and Wyoming.
